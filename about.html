ABOUT ME

autobiography

I was born in the Russia and spent my childhood on the Kamchatka Peninsula, a remote region in the Far East. I studied in School with accelerated education program. When I was 14, I moved to Kaliningrad, which is on the opposite side of Russia. My new home turned out to be far away from ex one. I finished school with excellent grades, could win some Olympiads, and received numerous awards. I excelled in my final exams and was commended by the governor. At 18, I enrolled in the Mining University of St. Petersburg. I studied Oil and Gas Engineering. I continued to take part in physics and math competitions. While studying at the university, I decided to take a programming course as an extracurricular activity. By that time, I had experience in programming in Pascal and C (obtained in school). I always approached programming without enthusiasm, but I was always good at writing code. So, I decided to try programming in Python. That was the moment that changed my life. Attempting to engage in programming as a hobby, rather than a mandatory discipline, has shown me how truly fascinating it can be. I easily passed this course. After finishing my second year, I decided to transfer to another university to continue my IT development and devote more time to it. I have applied to the Easter Mediterranean University in Cyprus. As a result, I am currently studying computer science. I am learning C, SQL, and Web programming. Moreover, I continue to learn Python. Furthermore, I have become interested in Data Science and am now studying this field. I am most interested in the field of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence.
